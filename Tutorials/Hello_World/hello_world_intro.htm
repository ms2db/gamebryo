Hello World: Introduction

Hello World is a simple application that introduces you to the basic concepts and components of the Emergent Game Framework.

In this section, we include a tutorial that walks you through the process of creating a simple Game Framework application like Hello World. In the process, the various parts of the Hello World application are described. We also discuss why these pieces are used and how they fit into the Game Framework. We also include a description of the Hello World demo itself, including an overview of how it works, its controls, and its principal source, script, and configuration files.
