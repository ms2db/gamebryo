InsideMadLabCubeMap.max

This 3ds Max sample illustrates how to create a cubic environment map in Max that is exported out to Gamebryo. The image below shows the Reflect/Refract map applied to the inside of the sphere. The camera is positioned inside the sphere and spins around its pivot point to create a panorama only possible with a cubic environment map.

By default, this sample installs to the following location:

%EMERGENT_PATH%\Samples\Models\Max Artist Examples\InsideMadlabCubeMap.MAX
