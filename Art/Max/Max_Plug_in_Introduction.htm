3ds Max Plug-in: Introduction

Welcome to Gamebryo 3ds max Plug-in. This manual gives you information, hints, and tips on how to produce great content using the Gamebryo 3ds max Plug-in inside 3D Studio Max.

The manual provides background information useful for you to decide about geometric limitations, graphics card compatibility, which visual effects work best, and the best way to achieve an overall pleasing result. To maximize your ability to create great content, it is a good idea to read the Artist's Guide cover to cover, but it is also useful for quick reference.
