Trees Ablend vs ATest 01.max

The Trees Ablend vs ATest 01.max sample demonstrates different ways to apply transparency on foliage, specifically focusing on the more expensive Alpha blending vs. Alpha testing, which sorts more easily and is faster.

Trees Ablend vs ATest 01.max sample

This sample illustrates that alpha testing can combine features of both alpha testing and alpha blending. The pixels that fail the alpha test are skipped completely. The pixels that remain are alpha blended into the scene to alleviate the black halo problem. Of all of these samples above, the one tagged 'Ablend + Atest' looks the best, performs moderately well, and sorts nicely.

The Black halo problem is also shown to varying degrees as the top row of branches all use a map that has black in the RGB channel. Conversely, the bottom set of branches all use a map the has green in the RGB channel. We recommend the bottom method for best results.
