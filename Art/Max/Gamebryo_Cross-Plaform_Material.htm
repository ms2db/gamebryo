Gamebryo Cross-Platform Material

This material has been designed to allow artists to specify completely different materials for each platform they wish to support. In some cases this may mean using different multi-texture tricks or different textures. The material displayed in the viewport can be selected using the Viewport radio buttons. Additionally, there are buttons on the standard Gamebryo toolbar that assist in easily switching all active Cross-Platform materials to a specific platform.
