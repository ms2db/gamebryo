Billboard.max and BillboardUp.max

A billboard is a texture that always faces the camera. It will rotate to always face the viewer, appearing volumetric or round. This effect is mainly used for light halos or completely round objects that look the same from all angles. The billboard.max sample demonstrates this technique. It includes a simple billboard that is designed to look like the volumetric scattering of light around a point light source. By default, this sample installs to the following location:

%EMERGENT_PATH%\Samples\Models\Max Artist Examples\billboard.max

Billboard sample

A second billboard example, billboardUp.max, has the Y-axis locked. In this case, you can still see perspective up and down, but it follows the camera horizontally. You define the billboard mode in Max via the object's "User Defined Properties". By default, this sample installs to the following location:

%EMERGENT_PATH%\Samples\Models\Max Artist Examples\billboardUp.max
