Gamebryo MaxScript Utilities Introduction

There are several provided MaxScripts that can make your life easier. These scripts can be found in the scripts\Gamebryo and scripts\startup folders, once you've installed the Gamebryo 3ds max Plug-in.

In order to access these utilities, the following steps must be taken:

  1. Open the Utilities panel.
  2. Open the MaxScript Utility.
  3. Click on the Utilities dropdown list.
  4. Click on the name of the utility you wish to use. Make sure to click on the actual text itself.

The utility rollout you selected will appear.
