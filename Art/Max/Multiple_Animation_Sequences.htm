Multiple Animation Sequences

A character often has many different animations. If you keep your character's animations in separate Max files, this can become difficult to manage. Not only do you have to keep track of many different files, but if you ever want to change the character model, you must change it in every Max file. Though at first it may seem difficult to set up, keeping all your character's animations in one Max file is easier to manage in the long run. Generally, we recommend that you create all your animations in one file.
