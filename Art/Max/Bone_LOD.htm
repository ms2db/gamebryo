Bone LOD.max

Bone LOD is a level of detail technique that combines the use of different skins with a subset of bones to increase run-time performance. In this 3ds Max sample, there are two distinct versions of the skin and four distinct subsets of bones. In Max, you can use the BoneLOD Utility to experiment with the settings of this file. You can preview the Bone LOD settings in Gamebryo by using the Animation Tool preview.

By default, this sample installs to the following location:

%EMERGENT_PATH%\Samples\Models\Max Artist Examples\Bone LOD.max
