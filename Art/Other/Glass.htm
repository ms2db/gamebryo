Glass

<Glass.NSF>

Requirements

  • NSF Shader Library
  • Vertex Shader 1.1 and Pixel Shader 2.0

Description

This shader implements ATI's Complex Glass effect, found in ATI's RenderMonkey 1.0 sample. It uses HLSL shaders. This shader is used for the glassy teapot in the ShaderSample demo.

Art Requirements

No additional art requirements beyond the Custom Attributes.

Custom Attributes

  • baseColor—The base material color for the object.
  • refractionScale—How much the reflection will contribute to the final effect.
  • reflectionScale—How much the refraction will contribute to the final effect.
  • indexOfRefractionRatio—The ratio between the material's index of refraction and the air's. The higher this number, the more the material will bend the light.
  • ambient—the ambient light in the scene.
  • rainbowSpread—How strongly the material refracts the light into separate colors. This allows the shader to create a rainbow effect around the edges of the object. The lower the value, the wider the rainbow effect will appear.
  • rainbowScale—How much the rainbow effect will contribute to the final effect.
  • EnvMap—A cubic environment map that will be used for reflection and refraction.
  • RainbowMap—A 1D texture that will be used to provide the color for the rainbow effect.
