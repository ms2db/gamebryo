Exporting Cameras from Maya

The Gamebryo Maya plug-in exports both perspective and orthographic cameras from Maya. In both export scenarios, the plug-in preserves the camera's frustum, though currently you can only animate the camera's position and orientation.

The following attributes are included when the plug-in exports a perspective camera:

  • Angle of View
  • Near Clip Plane
  • Far Clip Plane

The following attributes are included when the plug-in exports an orthographic camera:

  • Orthographic Width
