Portals with Maya

The Gamebryo Maya plug-in supports a mechanism for visibility culling known as portals. The general artist notes for this capability are located in the documentation for the Portal Converter plug-in.

For your convenience, a number of portal system-related buttons are on the Gamebryo shelf in Maya. These buttons enable you to create:

  • room groups
  • rooms
  • walls
  • two-way portals

Other buttons enable you to hide/show walls and portals. Check out "Portals.mb" to see an example of using the portal system.
