Introduction to Animation with Maya

Gamebryo exports animation just as you see it in Maya. This section primarily exists to provide artists with additional details about animation export where it is needed.

Animation export is made more powerful with the notion of sequences. Sequences are snippets of an animation that are written to a file. The pages in this section of the documentation discuss how to extract animation sequences to *.KF (Key Frame) sequence files for Gamebryo to use.
