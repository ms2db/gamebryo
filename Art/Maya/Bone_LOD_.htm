Bone LOD

Bone LOD enables you to have LOD control of skinned characters. The principle is simple and similar to regular LOD. Using the Bone LOD system, you can describe the different LOD level of bones that will affect a skinned object.

Bone LOD button

At the highest level of detail, you may want all the bones to affect the skin. Successive levels of detail involve fewer bones, so they will update faster. You can use Bone LOD to specify a bone that will act as the leaf bone in a hierarchy and no updates will propagate to its children. Using the classic character example, the base character is for close up shots and cut scenes and uses the full set of bones for facial expressions, fingers, toes, hair, etc. The next level of detail might cut out facial bones and hair bones. At the highest level of detail, the example shows detail bone control of the lips and eyebrows. At the next level of detail, the jaw and eyebrows are just single bones. At the lowest level of detail, all facial expression disappears, and there is a single bone for the skull.

Bone LOD works in combination with Skin LOD. You can use one without the other.
