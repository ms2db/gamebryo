Glow Map

The "Glow Map" is an RGB map that defines sections of the object that are self-lit. Black areas of this texture have no effect, while brighter areas will appear to glow. This map is useful in glowing effects like lit windows on a building or flashing indicators on a control panel for example.

The glow map is added late in the texturing pipeline—it will tend to "glow through" most other effects. Fully saturated colors will maintain their saturation, while pure black has no effect on the base texture.
