Toggle Portal Walls and Portals

Click the Toggle Portal Walls and Portals button to streamline the process of creating portal systems for Gamebryo.

Toggle Portal Walls and Portals button

When you click this button, Gamebryo portals and walls will have their "Visibility" flags toggled between "ON" and "OFF". If nothing is selected, then ALL portals and walls will be toggled; otherwise, the portals and walls of the selected objects and their children will be toggled. Look in the Maya Artist Examples directory of the Gamebryo installation for examples of using the portal system.

Note

In general you will want to hide all the portals and walls associated with your scene. If you do not hide them, they will be visible in your scene when you export it.
