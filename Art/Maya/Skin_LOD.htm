Skin LOD

Click the Skin LOD button for enhanced control over skinned objects. You can use Skin LOD to determine what skins are associated with different Bone LOD levels. Just as you can associate different groups of bones with a particular LOD, you can associate a skin with Bone LOD levels as well. The highest LOD may have a complex skin, but you might want to associate a simpler mesh with the lower levels of detail to further simplify drawing. It is important to remember that Bone LOD and Skin LOD work together. If you are going to use either, you need to use both.

Note

It is very important to remember that Bone LOD levels start at Zero (0) as the highest level of detail. This may seem backwards because Level 0 is the highest level of detail but this was done for performance reasons so please keep the level numbering system in mind.
