Spawn On Death

The Gamebryo Maya Plug-in extends Maya's functionality by adding support for spawning new particles when a particle dies. Click the Create Emitter button to add the necessary "Extra Attributes" to the particles shape.

Create Emitter button

The newly spawned particles can come from the currently existing particle system, or they can come from an entirely different particle system.
