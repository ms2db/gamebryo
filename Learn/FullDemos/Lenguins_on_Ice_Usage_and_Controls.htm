Lenguins on Ice Sample Usage and Controls

General Usage

  • When the demo starts, it renders a splash screen while loading. After the loading is complete, the start menu will appear, enabling the user to start a new game or quit.
  • The game will run for 3 minutes, during which the player is expected to try to prevent the baby lenguins from being hit or pushed off the edge by ice blocks. After the 3 minute time limit, the player's score will be displayed, and then the player will have the opportunity to play again. The player's score is a function of how many babies are left alive and how many ice blocks have been broken (more is better on both counts).
Keyboard Game Pad Action
W/w Left Analog Moves the Momma away from the camera
S/s Left Analog Moves the Momma towards the camera
A/a Left Analog Moves the Momma to the left of the camera.
D/d Left Analog Moves the Momma to the right of the camera.
Q/q Top Left Trigger Strafes the Momma to the left.
E/e Top Right Trigger Strafes the Momma to the right.
Mouse Right Analog Controls camera/Momma look at direction, i.e. yaw and pitch for left-right and up-down mouse movement, respectively.
Space A Button Makes the Momma jump.
Enter B Button Toggle in and out of Panic mode.
Mouse Wheel or '.' and ',' Bottom Right and Left Triggers Slides the camera closer (roll wheel forward) and further away from the Momma (roll wheel backwards).
UP_ARROW, DOWN_ARROW LEFT_UP, LEFT_DOWN Prev/Next menu item (when menu is active)
LEFT_ARROW, RIGHT_ARROW LEFT_LEFT, LEFT_RIGHT Dec/Inc menu item value (when menu is active)
ESC Start Button Quit or toggle menu on/off, depending on build.
