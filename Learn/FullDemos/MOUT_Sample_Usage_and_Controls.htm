MOUT Sample Usage and Controls

Control Action
Keyboard
ESC Exit application.
W/w Move character in his facing direction.
S/s Move character backwards.
A/a Sidestep left.
D/d Sidestep right.
F1 Wireframe for main scene components.
F/f Toggle frame rate.
Mouse—Controls camera/character look at direction (in other words, yaw and pitch for left-right and up-down mouse movement) respectively.
Left mouse button When depressed, the camera will smoothly zoom in to a "telephoto" view. When the button is released, the view will zoom back to the default "wide angle" view.
Mouse wheel Slides the camera closer to the character (roll wheel forward) and further away from the character (roll wheel backwards). Sliding the camera very close to the character will cause the view to switch to first-person mode
