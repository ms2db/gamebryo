Product Tour

The Product Tour offers an overview of the major tools and workflows that Gamebryo LightSpeed provides. The tour is fairly high-level and provides information for all members of a development studio (artists, designers, programmers, and so on). After you've completed the tour, you'll be familiar with the major terms used in the product. You'll also understand how the LightSpeed tools interoperate to help you build your game.

The tour takes around thirty minutes to complete and comprises the following sections:

  • Product Tour: Data Driven Games
  • Product Tour: Editing Levels
  • Product Tour: Editing Game Objects
  • Product Tour: Editing Behaviors
  • Product Tour: Editing 3D Content
