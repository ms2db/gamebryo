Purpose of the CollisionTestStatic Demo

This sample demonstrates:

  • Use of all the alternate bounding volume (ABV) proxy types, except unions, in a simple example of Gamebryo's collision detection system.
  • Use of Colliders and Collidees in NiCollisionGroups.
  • Use of a "static" collision approach, whereby the application does not furnish a velocity vector to the collision detection system.
