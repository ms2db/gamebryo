ShaderSample Platform Considerations

There are a number of platform-specific considerations and differences that apply to the ShaderSample demo.

DirectX 9

The ShaderSample demo demonstrates a wide diversity of support for shaders in DirectX 9.

Direct3D 10 and 11

The D3D10/D3D11 version of the shaders in ShaderSample have been rewritten in HLSL where necessary to reflect the fact that D3D10 does not support assembly shaders. In addition, to show off the use of the geometry shader in various situations, the D3D10 shaders all have had geometry shaders added that modify the effects. There is also a D3D10/D3D11-only effect that shows various ways of accessing the D3D10/D3D11 stream out capability.

On the Direct3D 10 and 11 platforms, Gamebryo does not support using shader programs written in nVidia's Cg .

Xbox 360

The ShaderSample demo demonstrates a wide diversity of support for shaders on Xenon (the Xbox 360).

Playstation 3

Support for the ShaderSample demo on Playstation 3 is currently limited to the DolphinTween and Glass effects. None of the ShaderLibrary based effects are currently supported.
