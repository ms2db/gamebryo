Signals and Slots

The NiSample framework takes advantage of the signals and slots architecture of the NiUserInterface library. This allows an application to easily bind the actions taken by interaction with UI elements with functional behavior specified by the application logic. The following slots are specified by NiSample:

  • m_kHideAll - Bound to NiSample::HideAllPressed
  • m_kToggleNavHelp - Bound to NiSample::ToggleNavHelp
  • m_kChangeController - Bound to NiSample::ChangeController
  • m_kQuit - Bound to NiSample::QuitApplication
