NiD3D10ErrorShader

: public NiD3D10Shader

<NiD3D10ErrorShader.h>

Description

NiD3D10ErrorShader is a special error shader meant to clearly identify when a shader otherwise fails to be applied to an object. The NiD3D10ErrorShader will oscillate 10% of the radius of the geometry's bounding volume, and will randomly change colors every frame. This behavior is very irritating to look at, and should immediately visually identify when there a problem.
