Fixtures

"Fixtures" is a general term applied to the rendered objects associated with a room, rather than special objects of a particular class. Objects such as the walls, ceiling, floor, furniture, columns, and light fixtures are all examples of fixtures. During culling, the fixtures in a given room are processed after the portals of that room are processed, but before the room returns from its OnVisible routine. In this way, fixtures added as children of the room that contains them appear in the resulting visible array before objects inside rooms that are closer to the viewer. All rooms have fixtures, as all rooms must contain wall geometry itself.
