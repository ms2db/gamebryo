Walls

It is important to note that, in order to achieve correct rendering (and the limited depth complexity described in this document), a wall containing a portal must have a "hole" cut out of the wall geometry where the portal is to go. One should not think of the portal as cutting a hole in the wall, but only as a representation of a connection between rooms.
