NiSphereBV

: public NiBoundingVolume

<NiSphereBV.h>

Description:

A point and a radius specify a NiSphereBV. The NiSphereBV alternate bounding volume (ABV) is identical to NiBound, the engine's default automatically generated bounding sphere, in terms of both operation and definition, with two exceptions.

One exception is that a NiSphereBV enables an application to specify a potentially tighter fitting sphere, which will only be used for collision testing.

Also, the NiSphereBV may be tested against other types of ABVs, whereas in the case of NiBounds, the "other" bounding volume must first be converted to a NiSphereBV.

Constructors:

NiSphereBV(const float fRadius = 1.0f, const NiPoint3& kCenter = NiPoint3::ZERO)

This constructor builds a sphere bounding volume with its default center at [0, 0,0] and a radius of one unit.

NiSphereBV(const NiSphereBV& kABV)

This copy constructor duplicates all the sphere parameters of the specified NiSphereBV.
