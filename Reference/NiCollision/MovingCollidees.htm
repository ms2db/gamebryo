Moving Collidees

Moving collidees should be specified as colliders. If they only move in rare instances, you may want to add them as collidees to the NiCollisionGroup initially, and then remove them and re-specify them as colliders when necessary. Such might be the case when you have a heavy box that can only be moved (becoming a collider) when the character is driving a bulldozer, but in the general case you want it to be immobile. So, when your character takes control of the bulldozer, you would change all the objects that are moveable by the bulldozer to colliders and, perhaps, change the collision callback.
