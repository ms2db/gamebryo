A few notes on this projection:

  • The 0.5 scalars in both rows map the projection volume from the range [-1.0,1.0] to the range [-0.5,0.5].
  • The 0.5 translations in the U and V directions are to map the projection volume from the range [-0.5,0.5] to the range [0.0, 1.0], which is the standard range for texture mapping.
