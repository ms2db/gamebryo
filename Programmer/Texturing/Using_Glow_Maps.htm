Using Glow Maps

Glow map on character, causing glowing tubes to appear on the surface of the character's body

  • Image: The image used is generally black in most regions, with medium-level colors in areas that are supposed to glow. Bright colors should be used carefully, since they will tend to wash out.
  • Filter: Any mode is reasonable, dependent upon the desired effect.
  • Clamping: Completely dependent upon the effect.
  • Texture Coordinates: Completely dependent upon the desired effect—glow map effects are as varied as base texturing.
