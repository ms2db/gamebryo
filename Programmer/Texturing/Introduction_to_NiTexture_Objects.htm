Introduction to NiTexture Objects

NiTexture and its subclasses, NiSourceTexture, NiRenderedTexture, and NiDynamicTexture, are perhaps the most pivotal classes in the overall Gamebryo texturing system. These three subclasses represent renderable image data in Gamebryo, and each of the three subclasses is used for specific, distinct effects. The following sections detail the types of image data that each object is capable of representing.

Two more advanced types of textures, NiSourceCubeMap and NiRenderedCubeMap are only supported as projected multitextures and only on some platforms—they are discussed in the documentation on Cube Mapping.
