Portals System

The Gamebryo portals module, NiPortal, is designed to enable applications to render and manage large interior datasets.  The module uses portal technology to cull the scene and geometry against not only the view frustum planes, but also against rough outlines of doorways and windows in the scene.  The figure below shows the simplest case.  The floor plan of a simple, two-room scene is shown.  If this scene were to be drawn by a standard scene graph renderer, both rooms would be drawn in their entirety, including drawing the occluded objects hidden behind the wall.  Using the portals module, the geometry is culled against planes defined by the doorway edges (right figure). This results in the culling of the hidden objects.
