Logging, Asserts, and Debuggers: Introduction

Logging, assertions and debuggers are the main means of getting insight into the running system. Using Gamebryo tools (in addition to ones you already use), you can diagnose problems, verify that the system is running as intended, and troubleshoot errors that may occur.
