Replication Prerequisites

Because replication is an extension of the entity system, we recommend that you familiarize yourself with that system before you dive into the details about replication. You should understand what entities are, how they work, and what their properties are. We also recommend that you acquaint yourself with best practices for working with entities. Specifically, you should read through the following documentation sections:

  • Entities Overview
  • How entities work
  • Entity properties overview
  • Best practices for working with entity properties

The eon::ReplicationService, which implements most of the replication functionality, interacts with the egf::EntityManager and the egf::Scheduler. So, it is helpful to understand these services as well.

Once you are familiar with these underlying concepts, then you are ready to further investigate LightSpeed's replication functionality.
