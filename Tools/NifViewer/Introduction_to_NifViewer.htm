NifViewer: Introduction

The NifViewer is a tool that enables you to preview Gamebryo assets on a console dev station. It enables you to load a NIF file, examine it, and play the animation. You can launch the tool from 3ds Max or Maya as a Gamebryo viewer, or you can launch it alone.
