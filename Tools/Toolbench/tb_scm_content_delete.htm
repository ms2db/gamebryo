Deleting Revision Controlled Content

When you delete content, it is immediately removed from Toolbench and marked for deletion in revision control. Unlike when you edit files, delete does not require you to save the files. Like other Toolbench revision control actions, deletes are only local. You still need to commit them using the revision control client. This means that deletes can be discarded easily.

To delete revision controlled content

  1. In Toolbench, right-click the content that you want to delete and choose Delete from the context menu.
  2. When you are ready to do so, use your revision control client to check in your changes. See Checking in Content to Revision Control.
