Converting Models to Presets

At any point in the development process, you may decide that a model is better represented by a preset. To make it easy for you, EMT allows you to convert models directly in the Mix-in and Diagram Editors. However, only models that do not have properties and behaviors assigned to them can be converted into presets.

Note

You can also convert presets into models.

To convert a model to a preset

  1. In the EMT Project Explorer, do one of the following:
    • Double-click a model to load it into the Mix-in Editor.
    • Double-click a diagram to load it into the Diagram Editor.
  2. Right-click the model that you want to turn into a preset and select Convert To Preset from the menu.

    The model is now a preset.
