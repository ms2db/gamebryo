Removing Models and Presets from the Diagram Editor

At any point in the game development process, you may want to clean up a diagram of unnecessary models/presets. To do so, you can easily remove one or more of the boxes that represent the models and presets from the editor. When you remove a model from a diagram, the model or preset is not deleted from the project.

Note

The Remove from diagram option is not available in the Mix-in Editor.

To remove a model or preset from the Diagram Editor

  1. In the Diagram Editor, right-click the box of the model or preset that you want to remove from the diagram.
  2. From the context menu, click Remove From Diagram.

    The box is removed from the diagram.
